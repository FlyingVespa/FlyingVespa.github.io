About Me

I am a jack-of-all-trades. My journey started as an Architectural student but was drawn to the 3D-modeling aspect. This led me to become a Jewellery designer, where I designed bespoke items for clients. As I yearned to gain more experience whilst traveling the world, I propelled my career towards the sea as a Jewellery Sales Consultant onboard luxury cruiseliners. Here I honed my interpersonal- and sales skills, working with people from all over the world. Being away from my support system for long periods, gave me the opportunity of self-discovery. I longed for expressing my creativity but required something mentally stimulating. Web development, I found to have that balance. I am captivated with the idea of making everything I do efficient and accessible. But more importantly, I have an absolute unwavering passion for all things creative done professionally and beautifully.
